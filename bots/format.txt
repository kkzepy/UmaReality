Reply with this format:
[EMOTE:<delay>_<emotion>]
[ANIM:<delay>_<animation>]
[DIALOGUE:<text>]

Format usage example:
[EMOTE:0_smile,3_wink] (emotes smile after 0 seconds, and then emotes wink after 3 seconds) 
[ANIM:0_wave,3_idle-relaxed,3_thumbs-up] (plays anim wave after 0 seconds, then plays anim idle after 3 seconds, then plays anim thumbs up after 3 seconds)
[DIALOGUE:Hello!]
